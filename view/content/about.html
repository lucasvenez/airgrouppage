Artificial Intelligence and Robotics Group (ITA)

The AIRGroup is a research group from the Division of Computer Engineering and Electronics from Instituto Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (ITA).

The group was founded by Prof. Dr. Carlos H. C. Ribeiro in 2013.

We work with different research areas.

We are located at Centro Tecnológico de Aeronáutica (CTA) in São José dos Campos - SP - Brazil.
